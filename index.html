Sip Programming Language

Sip is like the cool kid of system-level programming languages—sharp, safe, and seriously fun. Forget the "mystical magic" of other languages; Sip keeps it real with no-nonsense clarity and full control. Static typing? Check. Explicit nil-checking? Double check. Seamless C interoperability? Oh, you bet. It's like having a Swiss Army knife for systems programming, but way cooler and without the sharp edges.

Note: Sip is still under heavy development , but stay tuned!
